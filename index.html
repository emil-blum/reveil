WHAT IS THIS

Every data point you see was collected the moment you arrived — no hacking, no phishing. This is what your browser broadcasts passively to every website, every day. Reveil simply shows it back to you.

YOUR DATA STAYS WITH YOU

Nothing is sent to any server or stored anywhere. Camera and microphone streams are processed locally — never encoded or transmitted. The only external call is to ipapi.co to resolve your IP location. You can verify everything by viewing the page source.
