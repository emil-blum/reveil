WHAT IS THIS

Reveil is a privacy awareness project. Every data point you see on this screen was collected automatically the moment you visited — no hacking, no phishing, no malware. This is the information your browser broadcasts passively to every website you visit, every day.

YOUR DATA STAYS WITH YOU

Nothing shown here is sent to any server, stored in any database, or shared with anyone. All processing happens entirely in your browser, on your device. This project has no backend. You can verify this by inspecting the page source. The only external call made is to ipapi.co to resolve your IP location — the same service used by millions of websites to display location-relevant content.
