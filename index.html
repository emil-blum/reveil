WHAT IS THIS

Every data point you see was collected the moment you arrived — no hacking, no phishing, no malware. This is the information your browser broadcasts passively to every website you visit, every day. Reveil simply shows it back to you.

YOUR DATA STAYS WITH YOU

Nothing shown here is sent to any server, stored in any database, or shared with anyone. All processing happens in your browser, on your device. Camera and microphone streams are processed locally and never transmitted. The only external call made is to ipapi.co to resolve your IP location — standard practice used by millions of websites. You can verify everything by viewing the page source.
